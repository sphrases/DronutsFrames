Babyhawk Frame


I designed a new frame for the emax babyhawk, due to its heavy vibrations and general lack of stability.

The frame is designed to be cut out of 1-2mm carbon or glass fibre plastic.
Personally, I would recommend 2mm for the base plate and 1mm for the two top plates.

What you need:

  • A cnc router
  • 8 little standoffs and screws (3mm nylon/plastic is recommended)
  • The dxf files (can be downloaded below)
  • An emax babyhawk or simmilar quadcopter
  • The 3d printed camera mount
